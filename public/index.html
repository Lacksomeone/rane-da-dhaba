Our Story

About Rane Da Dhaba

A humble dhaba with the bold flavors of Lucknow, right in the heart of Bahraich

Where Taste Meets Tradition

Located at Shop No. 7-8, Near Nagar Palika Market on Kachehry Road in Brahmnipura, Bahraich — Rane Da Dhaba has become the go-to spot for anyone craving authentic North Indian and Mughlai non-vegetarian cuisine.

Our recipes are inspired by the legendary culinary traditions of Lucknow. Every dish is slow-cooked with hand-ground spices, fresh ingredients, and a generous dose of love. From our signature Butter Chicken to the aromatic Mutton Biryani, each plate is a journey through the royal kitchens of Awadh.

We believe great food doesn't need a fancy setting — just honest cooking and fresh ingredients. That's the Dhaba way. 🍗
